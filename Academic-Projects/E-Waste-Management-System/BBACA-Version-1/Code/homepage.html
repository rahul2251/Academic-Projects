WELCOME TO GREEN DREAM E-WASTE MANAGEMENT

Electronic waste or e-waste is any broken or unwanted electrical
or electronic appliance. E-waste includes computers, entertainment
electronics, mobile phones and other items that have been discarded
by their original users. E-waste or electronics recycling is the process
of recovering material from old devices to use in new products.The main
purpose of online e-waste management system is to provide another
way for the customer to giving the e-waste material. The E-waste
management system is the Website that can be accessed throughout the
Net and can be accessed by anyone who has a net connection.
