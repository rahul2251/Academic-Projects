About GreenCycle

Proudly Serving Pune & PCMC

Mission: Zero Landfill

India generates over 3.2 million tonnes of e-waste annually. At GreenCycle, our mission is to ensure none of that ends up poisoning our soil or water.

We are a CPCB-authorized recycler committed to ethical disposal. We value transparency, environmental safety, and convenience for our users.

  • Govt. Authorized & ISO Certified
  • 100% Transparent Process

Our Services

Comprehensive e-waste management solutions for homes and businesses.

Home Pickup

Convenient doorstep collection. Schedule a slot via our app, and our verified agents will handle the rest.

Corporate Collection

Bulk disposal solutions for IT parks and offices in Pune. Includes Form-6 compliance documentation.

Data Destruction

Your privacy matters. We offer on-site degaussing and physical destruction of hard drives.

Refurbishing

Extending the life of usable electronics. We refurbish gadgets to donate to underprivileged schools.
