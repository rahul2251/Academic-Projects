About GreenCycle


Committed to responsible and transparent e-waste recycling across Pune & PCMC.

Pune & PCMC

Mission: Zero Landfill

India produces over 3.2 million tonnes of e-waste every year. GreenCycle exists to ensure that electronic waste is handled responsibly—without harming our soil, water, or air.

We are a CPCB-authorized recycling organization focused on transparency, environmental protection, and convenient doorstep collection for citizens and businesses.
